-SHORTCUTS:-
------------

[1-0]                   recall preset [1-10]

opt-[a|s|d|f|g|h|j]     cycle through pedal positions
a|s|d|f|g|h|j           increment pedal position
S-[a|s|d|f|g|h|j]       decrement pedal position

------------

S       = shift
opt     = option
